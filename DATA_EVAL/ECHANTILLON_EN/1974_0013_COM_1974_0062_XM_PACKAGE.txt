Proposal for a Regulation of the Commission fixing sluice-gate prices and levies for eggs
The annexed proposal is based on Articles 3 and ^ of Council Regulation nr. I22/67/EEC on the Common Organisation of the market in Eggs. The rules for the calculation of levies and sluice-gate prices applicable for eggs wore laid down in Council Regulation 145/67/EEC. The methods of calculation have been specified in the recitals of the proposal.
