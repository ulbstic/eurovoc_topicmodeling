It also represents, in certain cases, the outside limit acceptable to Member States beyond which, leaving aside the fact that an undesirable precedent . would be created, the advantages accruing from an arrangement with Greece would not be worth negotiating.This draft will be firmly contested by certain Member States but it could, eventually, find acceptance if presented above all as a final attempt.
