On the basis of Protocol n° 5 annexed to the Lome II Convention the Council opened for 198V by means of Regulation ( EEC) n° 1700/ 81 a Commu- nity tariff quota for the import into the Community of Nine of 189 029 hi of rum, arrack and tafia originating in the ACP States.
By Regulation ( EEC) n° 439 / 81 of the Council laying down the provisional arrangements applicable to trade between the Hellenic Republic and the ACP States-, Greece is required to apply this tariff measure. An amendment to the abovementioned regulation is therefore required.
In accordance with the provisions of Protocol n° 5 annexed to the Lome II Convention imports during 1980 have been taken into consideration as the basis of calculating the volume of the tariff quota.
Since Greece imported in 1980 41.58 hectolitres of pure alcohol of the products in question the volume of the quota must be increased by 41 hi + 18 % = 49 hi. This quantity is to be allocated to Greece as its quota- share .
